FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my bank account?

Go to Financial Tracker → Bank Connections → Select your bank and follow the secure OAuth process.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and never store your login credentials. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

How often does the app sync?

By default, syncing happens in real-time. You can adjust this in Settings → Sync Preferences.

Can I export my data?

Yes, go to Settings → Data Management → Export Data to download your information in various formats.
